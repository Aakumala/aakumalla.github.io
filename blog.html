16thANNIVERSARY CELEBRATION IMAGE Himayatnagar.

IMAGE CREATIVE EDUCATION Himayatnagar celebrated its 16th Anniversary in a grand manner.
This year also, Anniversary celebration environment was inititated with events that bring together all students and staff onto the same platform by conducting indoor games as well as outdoor games like Carroms, chess, one-minute games, Cricket for boys and throwball & shuttlecock for girls.
Few former students shared their interview and work experiences which infused confidence in the current students.
Their testimonials have truly encouraged others.
Our Managing Director Mr. Vijaygopal Reddy garu and Mrs. Sudathi Madam spoke on the occassion by encouraging the students to reach new heights and be role models for others.
The Anniversary Event day was marked by vibrant dance and song performances by the students.
This is the day usually everyone waits for, as this event creates an opportunity to know about one another, about the Industry - its scope and opportunities.
The event concluded by cutting New Year cake.
Wishing everyone a Happy New Year 2017.
