About us

IMAGE HIMAYATNAGAR / MALAKPET ALUMNI ASSOCIATION was started in the year 2011 by a group of likeminded former students with a sole motive of creating awareness on the various courses offered by the institute and the career options avaible after completing the course at IMAGE.

  • Alumni meet usually goes hand-in-hand with our Institute Anniversary that is celebrated in a big way every year, during the last week of December.
  • But now as the demand for Careers in Multimedia and Animation is growing in leaps and bounds that no one has anticipated, we have comeup with this website that would be more helpful for the Multimedia / Animation / VFX enthusiasts.
  • We expect that this initiative would be of great help to a larger audience on the whole.
  • We wish to address a variety of areas, such as academic, careers, mentorship, projects, etc.
  • We also invite all former students who have pursued their interests in IMAGE MALAKPET & HIMAYATNAGAR to join the association and share your knowledge and experience, which would surely help in guiding the aspiring Designers, Animators, VFX artists.

Image Himayatnagar is one of the most experienced Training Institute in Hyderabad with over 16 years of industry presence. A knowledge power house, that has been fueling the growth of Digital Media. Established in 2000, We are pioneer in the field of Digital Media Education and Digital Media Content development.

A pioneer in the field of Multimedia Training.
